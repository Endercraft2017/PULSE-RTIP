Program Functionality
"The purpose of the project is to improve disaster preparedness and emergency response by providing an efficient and accurate eay for citizens to report incidents using text images, videos, and GPS location, the report will be send directly to the municipal disaster risk reduction and management office (MDRRMO) for validation and quick response. The project aims to evaluate the system in terms of functionality, usability, and reliability. The expected result of the study is that the developed system will be rated as highly acceptable and effective in improving communication and response during emergencies.

4. Provide additional incident categories including an "others" option.

6. Notify user when thier reports are received or rejected, including the reason for rejection.
7. Implement a color-coded dashboard for clearer data visualization.
8. Develope a community board where users can post incidents publicly or privately.
9. Add an offline mode for reporting.
